How does Meet & Greet parking work?

Booking and parking with Meet & Greet is quick and easy. Follow these simple steps for a seamless experience:

  • Book Your Parking: Reserve your Meet & Greet service.
  • Receive Instructions: The day before your travel, you'll receive a text message with full instructions.
  • Arrival at the Airport:
    • Drive to the Orange Short Stay Car Park.
    • Your driver will be waiting to greet you.
  • Returning from Your Trip:
    • Once through passport control, head back to the same drop-off point.
    • Your car will be ready and waiting for you.
Enjoy the convenience of our Meet & Greet service, ensuring a smooth and stress-free start and end to your journey.

Directions to Orange Short Stay (Drop Off Point)

  1. Set Google Maps to London Stansted Airport
  2. From the North:
    • Leave the M11 at J8.
  3. From the South:
    • Leave the M11 at J8a.
  4. Follow the Signs:
    • Follow the signs for London Stansted Airport to the Bassingbourn roundabout.
  5. Bassingbourn Roundabout:
    • Take the 2nd exit onto Thremhall Avenue.
  6. Thremhall Avenue Roundabout:
    • Take the 3rd exit onto Terminal Rd S.
  7. Final Step:
    • Follow signs to Orange Premium Car Park.

With these clear steps, you'll reach the Orange Premium Car Park at London Stansted Airport without any hassle. Safe travels!
